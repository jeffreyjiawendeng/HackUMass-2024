Bright Side

This program uses machine learning to capture and analyze facial expressions via your device’s front camera to identify emotions like happiness, sadness, and anger. By studying facial features in real-time, the system provides insights into human emotional states, exploring the boundaries of AI in emotion recognition.

User privacy is a top priority—no images are stored or shared. Data is discarded immediately after analysis, and users must give explicit consent before any image capture occurs.

This technology can enhance applications in areas such as customer service, mental health monitoring, and education, while ensuring ethical standards and respect for user privacy.
